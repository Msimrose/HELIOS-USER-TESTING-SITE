Test the Helios app

Helios is a circadian lighting system. A smart lamp that shifts its brightness and color temperature throughout the day to match your natural rhythm. This is the iOS app that controls it.

You're testing the app interface only. No physical lamp needed, the app includes a live lamp render that responds to every change you make.

Install via TestFlight, try 6 tasks, answer a short questionnaire. 10 to 15 minutes, anonymous.

You'll need

iPhone, iOS 16+, 15 minutes.

Install Helios

Install via TestFlight
  1. 1
    Get TestFlight

    App Store, free, by Apple.

  2. 2
    Tap the button above

    Opens TestFlight with the Helios invite.

  3. 3
    Accept & install

    Tap Accept → Install.

  4. 4
    Open Helios

    You'll see the circadian arc and controls.

No physical lamp connected. The app simulates the light output on screen. You'll see a floating lamp widget in the corner that reacts to everything you do: scenes, brightness, color temperature.

You can drag the widget around and long-press it to resize.

Step 3 of 5 · Test

Tasks

Do these in the app. Watch the lamp render and floating widget react to each change.

  1. 1
    Power

    Turn the light on and off. Watch the lamp render respond.

  2. 2
    Brightness

    Drag the brightness slider. The lamp and widget glow update live.

  3. 3
    Colour temperature

    Slide between warm and cool. Notice the color shift.

  4. 4
    Scenes

    Try at least 3 presets. Each sets a different mood and brightness.

  5. 5
    Schedule

    Open the Schedule tab. Browse the phase cards from Sunrise to Night.

  6. 6
    Widget

    Drag the floating lamp widget to a different corner. Long-press to resize it.
